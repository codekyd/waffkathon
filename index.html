Warri Hackathon - Waffkathon.

Waffkathon is a three day hack aimed at providing solutions to internet crime,
environmental issues and accountability in public sector.

An Hackathon aimed at solving local and global issues.

Internet Security

Develop a lasting solution to solve the rate of internet crime in Nigeria.

Environmental Issues

Develop a lasting solution that will make our environment cleaner and greener for better health

Public Sector

Develop a lasting solution that will improve the quality of service delivery to the masses, whether by private or government institution.

Participate

The Process

1

Apply to Participate

Submit your application at waffkathon.com/submit or by clicking the PARTICIPATE button.

Mar. 2nd - 16th, 2019

2

Get accepted & join slack

Get acceptance email & log into your application to confirm you participation. Join slack channel after confirmation.

Mar. 17th, 2019

3

Slack party and bonding

Join the Waffkathon Slack team, create a private channel for your team.

Mar. 18th, 2019

4

Pick a project and form a team

create a team, come up with an idea or pick one from the ideas board.

Mar. 19th - 21st, 2019

5

Idea validation

Work with your team on your project. Validate your ideas and conclusions.

Mar. 22nd - 24th, 2019

6

Start Hacking

Start hacking and building your idea with your teammates.

Mar. 25, 2019, 7AM

7

wrapping up

Wrap up you solution for the hackathon.

Mar. 26, 2019

8

Demo, pitch & win

Demo your solutions. Prices are announced and given to winners

Mar. 27, 2019

9

incubation Program and Startup Launch.

After the event
