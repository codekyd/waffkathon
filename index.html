The Process

1

Apply to Participate

Submit your application at waffkathon.com/submit or by clicking the PARTICIPATE button.

Mar. 2nd - 16th, 2019

2

Get accepted & join slack

Get acceptance email & log into your application to confirm you participation. Join slack channel after confirmation.

Mar. 17th, 2019

3

Slack party and bonding

Join the Waffkathon Slack team, create a private channel for your team & start hacking.

Mar. 18th, 2019

4

Pick a project and form a team

create a team, come up with an idea or pick one from the ideas board.

Mar. 19th - 21st, 2019

5

Idea validation

Work with your team on your project. Ask mentors questions on slack. Ensure you push your changes regularly to Github.

Mar. 22nd - 24th, 2019

6

Start building

Submit your project on DevPost and include the github link.

Mar. 25, 2019, 7AM

7

wrapping up

Meet in Lagos Nigeria & listen to talks from speakers speaking from around the world. Be one of 100 teams to demo your project in expo-style setting to esteemed judges. Get your personalized NaijaHacks Goodie bag.

Mar. 26, 2019

8

Demo, pitch & win

Return to venue to pitch to VCs & Execs from Nigeria & Silicon valley. Get Official NaijaHacks T-shirt & more swag.

Mar. 27, 2019

9

incubation Program and Startup Launch.

After the event
